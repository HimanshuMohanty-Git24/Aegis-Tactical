# Project Brief: Nyayamitra AI

## Overview
Nyayamitra AI is an intelligent legal assistant built to democratize access to legal
information in India. The system uses natural language processing to help citizens
understand their legal rights related to consumer protection, labor law, and
fundamental rights under the Indian Constitution.

## Key Capabilities
- Natural language legal query resolution in English and Hindi
- Case precedent retrieval from Indian court databases
- Simplified explanation of complex legal provisions
- Document summarization for legal notices and contracts

## Architecture
- Frontend: React-based web application with responsive design
- Backend: Python FastAPI service with RAG pipeline
- Vector Store: Pinecone for legal document embeddings
- LLM: Amazon Bedrock (Claude 3.5 Sonnet) for reasoning

## Known Limitations
- System is advisory only — not a substitute for professional legal counsel
- Coverage limited to Indian federal law; state-specific statutes may vary
- Real-time case updates depend on court database API availability
- Hindi language support is partial (translations may lack legal precision)

## Project Status
- Version 1.2 released January 2026
- Active development ongoing
- 500+ test cases validated
- User feedback indicates 87% satisfaction rate for consumer protection queries
